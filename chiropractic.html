Chiropractic

In general
Chiropractic is designed to restore motion to spine and allow your body to heal in the best possible way.
‍
The spine
It is the science and art concerned with the relationship between the spinal column and the nervous system as it affects the restoration and maintenance of health, primarily utilizing the hands to adjust misaligned or malfunctioning vertebrae.
