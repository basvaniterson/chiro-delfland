What to expect

Welcome to Delfland Chiropractie! We are excited to meet you! To better understand your health concerns and best serve you, we will need a full health history.

When you arrive, you will be greeted by your doctor Nathan van Beelen, who will give you a short office tour so you can feel right at home.
‍
An in-depth consultation is conducted in order for the doctor to get a better picture of your overall health and discuss your specific health goals. The doctor will then do a focused exam on your spine as well as any other areas of complaint.

Once the doctor has a clear idea of what is going on, you will receive your first chiropractic adjustment. A chiropractic adjustment is designed to restore movement to your spine and allow your body to heal.
‍
After your first adjustment we will make a plan to help you achieve your health goals. At our practice we approach every patient on an individual basis and form a care plan based on your specific needs.
